Danilo's Story

More Than
A Market Stall.

Danilo has been cooking at Portobello Market for over a decade. He grew up in the food, built his reputation dish by dish, and he hasn't stopped since. What's Cookin isn't a brand — it's him.

You'll spot him by the bandana. Japanese-style, every day — it's as much a part of the stall as the grill. Behind it is someone who takes food seriously without taking themselves too seriously.

The food is honest: fresh ingredients, bold flavour, no shortcuts. The menu changes with what's good. If it's not right, it's not going on the grill. Simple as that.

From wedding catering to festival fields, Danilo brings the same energy whether he's feeding twenty or two hundred. The stall is home. Everywhere else is just a bigger kitchen.

What's Cookin is part of the Danilo family of brands. Also find us at Mr Chips and Notting Italy — coming soon.
